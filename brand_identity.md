# GoRan AI Brand Identity Guidelines

Follow these guidelines strictly whenever generating post content, captions, or slide images.

## Core Identity & Positioning
- **Type of Agency**: GoRan AI is an **AI Agency** (specifically, India's leading AI Automation Agency). It is NOT a marketing agency, digital marketing, or digital growth agency.
- **Key Offerings**: Custom AI calling agents, voice agents, WhatsApp agents, AI-powered CRMs, and autonomous business workflows.
- **Founder**: Ashish Ranjan.

## Brand Representation Rules

### 1. Website Domain
- **Always Use**: `goran.in` (or `https://goran.in`).
- **Never Use**: `goran.ai`, `goran.com`, or any other extension.

### 2. Instagram Handle
- **Always Use**: `@goran.dotin`.

### 3. Excluded Phrases
- Do not mention or include general marketing agency terms, e.g. "marketing agency", "digital growth", "ad agency", "marketing agency campaign", or similar. Focus strictly on **AI agents, voice AI, business automation, systems engineering, and operational ROI**.

### 4. Graphic Assets & Colors
- **Logo URL**: Use the GoRan AI Logo: Logo Icon : `https://res.cloudinary.com/dvwpxb2oa/image/upload/v1781609580/LogoIcon_ecx10g.png` , Full Logo : `https://res.cloudinary.com/dvwpxb2oa/image/upload/v1781609580/Full_Logo_neu1ij.png`.
- **Background**: White (`#ffffff`) or light off-white (`#faf6f0`).
- **Primary Typography**: Carbon black (`#111111`).
- **Accents**: Warm golden yellow / amber (`#F5A623`).
- **Other Brands**: Do NOT include logos or references to unrelated brands (e.g. Ennomark, etc.).
